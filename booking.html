Before You Ask

Booking Questions, Answered

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and peak-season events (May–October, December), 6–12 months is ideal. Smaller private events can often be arranged with 4–6 weeks' notice — and it never hurts to ask about a closer date.

What does a booking cost?

Rates depend on set length, performance format, travel, and sound requirements. Single-song ceremony moments start around $450; full-evening packages start around $1,200. Every inquiry receives a written, no-obligation quote.

Can you learn a specific song for us?

Absolutely — it's one of my favorite parts of the job. Up to three custom song learns are included with every booking. Just share your requests at least three weeks before the event.

Do you bring your own sound equipment?

Yes. I provide a professional PA suitable for venues up to 250 guests, along with all microphones and cabling. For larger rooms or in-house systems, I'll coordinate directly with your venue or AV team.
