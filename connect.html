For the Fans

A Few Ways to Stay Close

Request a Cover

There's a song you've always wanted to hear in my voice — I want to know what it is. Send requests through Instagram DMs or email hello@sydneysmall.com. I pick one fan request to record every month.

Join the Mailing List

A short, once-a-month note with new videos, public show dates, and the occasional unreleased demo. Email hello@sydneysmall.com with "Add me to the list" and you're in. No spam — I promise, I don't have the time.

Catch a Public Show

While most of my calendar is private events, I still play a handful of public lounge nights around Atlanta each season. Dates are always announced first on Instagram.

Planning Something Special?

Dates book quickly — especially spring and December weekends. If you have an event in mind, let's get your evening on the calendar.
